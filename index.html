Our Tour Plans

KASHI

Kashi, also known as Varanasi, is a city in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, revered as one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a major spiritual hub in Hinduism. Situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River, Kashi is renowned for its ancient temples, bustling ghats (riverfront steps), and vibrant culture.

BODHGAYA

Bodh Gaya, located in the Indian state of Bihar, holds immense spiritual significance as the place where Siddhartha Gautama, the Buddha, attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree over 2,500 years ago. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a major pilgrimage destination for Buddhists worldwide.

Book Now

TRIVENI SANGAM

Triveni Sangam, located in Prayagraj (formerly Allahabad), India, is the auspicious confluence point where three sacred rivers, the Ganges, the Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati, merge. Revered as one of Hinduism's holiest sites, millions of pilgrims visit annually to partake in sacred rituals and take a dip in the holy waters.

Book Now

VINDHYACHAL

Vindhyachal, nestled in the Vindhya Range of central India, is revered as a sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to the Hindu goddess Vindhyavasini. This tranquil town in the Mirzapur district of Uttar Pradesh draws devotees seeking blessings and spiritual solace.

Book Now

VISHNUPATH

Vishnupad Temple, located in the holy city of Gaya in Bihar, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It is believed to enshrine the footprint of Lord Vishnu, marking the spot where he is said to have placed his foot while calming the demon Gayasur.

Book Now

RAM MANDIR

Ram Mandir, situated in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, is a historic Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ram, a revered deity in Hinduism. The temple holds significant religious and cultural importance as it marks the birthplace of Lord Ram according to Hindu mythology.

Book Now

SWARVED TEMPLE

Swarved Mandir Varanasi, situated in the spiritual heart of India, is a divine sanctuary dedicated to inner awakening and self-realization. Established by Sant Pravar Sri Vigyan Saheb, the temple embodies the essence of Swarveda, an ancient spiritual text.

Book Now

SARNATH

Sarnath, located near Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh, India, is a revered Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha preached his first sermon after attaining enlightenment. This ancient city holds immense historical and spiritual significance, with its well-preserved ruins.

Book Now

CHITRAKOOT

Chitrakoot, nestled in the lap of nature in Madhya Pradesh, India, is a revered pilgrimage destination steeped in mythology and spirituality.This picturesque town is believed to be the place where Lord Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana spent significant part of their exile during the Ramayana era.
